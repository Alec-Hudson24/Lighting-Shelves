# Lighting-Shelves

**modern shelves next to bed, LED lights controlled with a ESP32 board connected to an app**  
*(Solo project: Computer aided design, wood working, problem solving)*

## Goal / Overview
- **Primary Requirements**:
  - Having more space in my room to display projects, legos, computer components.
  - Merge the theme of my dark wood color desk to my white bed.
- **Motivation**: I needed more space for the things I wanted to display in my room
- **Outcome**: shelves that display everything I want and improved woodworking skills, painting, lighting, and design.

## Programs and Skills Required
- **3D Modeling & Design**: SolidWorks and modeling my room and what I wanted to incorperate to my room
- **Manufacturing & Fabrication**: Skill saw, drills, painting, sanding
- **Team Collaboration**: communicating with manufacturers to cut the translucent backing to the shelves

## Key Takeaways & Lessons Learned
- Always think about tolerances, when creating the design in Solidworks I make the tolerance perfect, causing me to have to problem solve on how I would fit the shelves in my room
- Think about fabrication during the design phase to reduce future headaches
